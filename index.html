Hi!

I’m a 2nd year PhD student in the Computational Photography Lab at SFU under supervision from Prof. Yağiz Aksoy. My research interests are Machine Learning for VFX and image editing.

Before joining SFU, I spent a few years as a Postproduction Supervisor working on numerous theatrical and television productions and restorations.

I received my M.Eng. in Imaging and Media Technology from TH Köln in 2015 and my B.Eng. Audiovisual Media from Media University Stuttgart in 2011.
